# 🐍 The Phantom Eel — MitTech Corporation

## **1. Who They Are**

MitTech Corporation is the world's most powerful company —
the sole manufacturer and distributor of MitTech gloves,
the technology everyone uses and nobody questions.

On the surface they are a legitimate business.
Professional. Respectable. Essential.
They keep the economy running, they supply the world's most important tool,
and they have the quiet approval of every government that matters.

On the street, people who know call them the **Phantom Eel**.
Dangerous and slippery — always untouchable, always gone before anyone can prove anything.
Most people laugh the rumor off. That's fine. That's the point.

---

## **2. How They Got Here**

MitTech was not their invention.

Before the Collapse, a crafter bloodline developed the original MitTech glove —
a survival tool built for a dangerous world, embedded with recipes, reinforced with steel,
designed for people who needed every advantage possible.

The company found it. Took it. Took the recipes, the name, and the brand.
The original inventor was killed to close the loose end.
The crafter family had no recourse, no voice, no one willing to risk anything for them.

The company repackaged the technology for civilian use,
stripped out everything that made it a survival tool,
and sold it to the world as if they'd always owned it.

They have, in their own way, ever since.

---

## **3. How They Operate**

The Phantom Eel doesn't need to hide.

They commit crimes when needed — quietly, efficiently, without loose ends.
Not enough to alarm anyone. Not enough to outweigh their economic value.
The governments that know look the other way because the math is simple:
one company's old crimes versus the entire world's most used technology.
It's not even a hard call.

Insiders who leave and talk find that nobody believes them.
The company doesn't chase them down. They don't need to.
Respectability is a better shield than silence.

---

## **4. Their Presence in the World**

- **Main HQ** — a single dominant building, location TBD
- **Storefronts in every town** — selling, servicing, and maintaining Stolen MitTech
- **Government relationships** — formal, transactional, mutually beneficial
- **Street reputation** — the Phantom Eel rumor exists everywhere, believed nowhere

To most people, MitTech Corporation is just the glove company.
Boring. Essential. Always there.

---

## **5. Their Role in the Story**

The company doesn't know the player exists yet.

They haven't thought about the crafter bloodline in a long time —
that loose end was tied off before most of their current staff were born.

As the player progresses, small anomalies will surface.
Unknown recipe signatures. Unauthorized MitTech activity.
Someone will file a report. It will move up the chain slowly, bureaucratically.

And eventually someone important will decide it needs to be handled.
